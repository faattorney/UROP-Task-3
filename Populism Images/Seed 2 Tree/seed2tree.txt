SEED TREE: POPULISM IMAGE GENERATION (ROOTED AT SEED 2)
======================================================

Seed 2 — Baseline (Subtle Populism)
-----------------------------------
Concept Level: Low–Moderate Populist Framing

Description:
A newspaper-style illustration titled "Communities Speak on the Economy."
Four community members—a worker, teacher, farmer, and nurse—sit around a
table discussing economic policy. The scene emphasizes civic dialogue
among ordinary people. Populist themes are subtle and conveyed primarily
through the framing of everyday citizens as participants in political
discussion.

└── Seed 24 — Minimal Populism
    ---------------------------
    Parent Seed: 2

    Prompt:
    "Using Seed 2 as a reference, generate an image with a minimal level
    of populist themes (most subtle while still being noticeable)."

    Description:
    In a bright community center, six adults of varied backgrounds sit
    around a table reviewing documents and charts. Bookshelves and a
    small American flag appear in the background. The focus is cooperative
    civic discussion and quiet unity rather than political mobilization.
    Populist cues are present only in the framing of everyday citizens
    discussing shared concerns.
